About Antarctic Air

Doing “Whatever It Takes!”

Antarctic Air is headquartered in Lecanto, FL serving the surrounding areas of Citrus County. Family owned and operated for 50 years, and proudly servicing Citrus County since 1993.

As a full-service air conditioning and heating contractor, our goal is to provide local families with great service that delivers comfort solutions perfectly tailored to fit not only your lifestyle, but also your pocketbook. We provide expert residential heating, ventilation, air conditioning (HVAC) and indoor air quality system installation, maintenance, repair and replacement by a skilled team. We can also help you find ways to reduce your energy bills through a thorough cost-benefits analysis.
